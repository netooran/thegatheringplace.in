What we are

The Gathering Place is a farm-to-table community kitchen where food, sustainability, and human connection intertwine. We design our menus around what's growing in our farm plots and what's available from our network of local farmers, ensuring peak freshness and minimal food miles.

  • Community-powered: Local volunteers and food enthusiasts help tend our gardens and kitchen
  • Zero-waste commitment: On-site composting, thoughtfully portioned pre-orders, and mindful menu design
  • Living traditions: Workshops on fermentation, preserving, and indigenous cooking techniques
  • Conscious cooking: Menus that respect seasonal rhythms and support local food systems

Quick facts

Most of our ingredients come straight from our own farm plots or are sourced from small-scale farmers nearby. Located inside Kilukka Farms, Sanctity Ferme (Shoolagiri / Krishnagiri), just 1.5 hours from Bangalore. Open for pickups and intimate dinners.
